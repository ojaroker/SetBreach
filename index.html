Each year, colleges are required to publich the common data set(CDS) Our mission is to help you find the perfect college by searching the data sets and matching you with the right fit using our algorithm. The survey should take no longer than 5 minutes. Use our resources link to view the universities on US News, Niche, and other school websites.

To begin, please click on the "survey" link. You will answer specific questions, and we will match you to your best fit.


Universities are viewed as "those best four years" for most people. A successful college experience is not defined soley by the academic ranking a school has to provide; many times, it is defined by the people we live with and attend classes with. Factors that might seem insignificant to some students might be deal breakers to other students. Thats why Set Breach aims to match students to their best-fit college using only the factors that matter to the individual at hand.

The biggest challenge in this project was figuring out how we would pull the data from many colleges' Common Data Sets. We experimented with PDF and spreadsheet reading libraries in Java but realized we did not have enough time or experience to continue with them. Instead, we chose 12 colleges and manually saved data to a text file. The next step was to design an algorithm to rank colleges based on a point system from each question. For most questions, the number of points added to a college equals the absolute value of the difference between the college's actual answer index and the user's inputted answer number. After completing the back end, we worked on the survey website and the process of transferring the user's inputted answers to the Java program.
